Power And Energy Society

The Power and Energy Society (PES) is the world’s largest platform for brilliant minds to come together and share the latest technological developments in the electric power industry, for developing standards that guide the development and construction of equipment and systems, and for educating members of the industry and the general public. Members of the Power and Energy Society are the pioneers in this field and they progress by joining hands with this unique and outstanding association.

Mission

To be the leading provider of scientific and engineering information on electric power and energy for the betterment of society, and the preferred professional development source for our members.

About Our Chapter

RIT has an IEEE ‐ PES Student Chapter with student members of the Electrical and Electronics Engineering and Electronics and Communication Engineering branches. The chapter is very active and conducts an ample number of regular programs which benefit the member community. The members also attend the PES technical lectures conducted by the IEEE Student Bodies of other colleges. IEEE PES RIT SB Chapter will be formed officially in August, 2017. It organise technical, professional developmental and social activities, including weekly workshops, LED/Solar kit workshops, inter college workshops, activities like quizzes, as part of world energy conservation day celebrations, for students and young professionals. Volunteers are welcome to help organise or collaborate on activities and events and to share ideas to support our Chapter activities.
